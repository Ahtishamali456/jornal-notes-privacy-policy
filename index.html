Privacy Policy

Effective date: 29-05-2024

Thank you for using our QR Code Scanner App. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains what information our app collects, how we use it, and how we protect your information.

Information We Collect

Our app requires permission to access your device's camera solely for the purpose of scanning QR codes. The camera is used to capture and interpret QR codes, allowing you to extract the encoded information.

How We Use Information

The information captured by the camera during the QR code scanning process is used only for the immediate purpose of decoding the QR code. The extracted data is displayed to you within the app and is not stored, shared, or used for any other purpose.

Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. Our app does not store or transmit any data obtained from QR code scans. All processing is done locally on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this privacy policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
